# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Recruiters, engineering managers, and fellow builders looking for a highly capable Data Engineer who doesn't take themselves too seriously.

## Product Purpose
To showcase Omkaar's technical depth (Aramark, NPR) through a tactile, interactive digital scrapbook that feels like a personal moodboard rather than a corporate resume.

## Brand Personality
- **Effortless Whimsy**: Technically dense but visually light.
- **Nonchalant/Chill**: Lowercase-primary, relaxed tone, zero "tech-bro" energy.
- **Tactile/Analog**: Physical stickers, cutout pictures, gifs of interfaces, polaroids, and blueprints.

## Anti-references
- **The "Tech Bro" Aesthetic**: No neon glows, no "Iron Man" HUDs, no aggressive blue/purple gradients, no Inter font.
- **The Corporate Grid**: No standard 3-column card sections, no formal bullet points.
- **AI Slop**: No generic "modern/sleek" AI-generated look.

## Design Principles
- **Scattered but Purposeful**: Elements should feel tossed onto a desk but remain legible and interactive.
- **Technical as Texture**: Blueprints and logs are design elements, not just data.
- **Show, Don't Tell**: Use gifs of interfaces and "cutout" imagery to demonstrate skill instead of listing them.
- **Interaction is the Reward**: Everything should bounce, drag, or respond with physics.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Readable font sizes for body text (Satoshi).
- High contrast in light mode (Bone paper vs Charcoal ink).
- Dark mode toggle ("Lampshade") for light sensitivity.
